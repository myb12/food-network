About us

We are here to help you cook delightful recipes with not so much pressure but rather more happiness. We offer plans and cooking guidance for home cooks, by home cooks.

Food Network was established in 2021 by Mohammad Yasin as a home cooking online journal to record his family recipes. Today, Food Network has developed into a confided-in asset for home cooks with in excess of 3,000 tested recipes, guides, and meal plans, drawing more than 15 million readers every month from around the world.
